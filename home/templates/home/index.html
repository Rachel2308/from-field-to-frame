Holly Dawes

Finding her inspiration away from the hustle of everyday life, Holly enjoys long hikes across the hills of central England with her very enthusiastic, full-of-energy border collie. It’s on these adventures that she ‘sees’ her next creation, breathing life into it on her return home.

Her paintings, all in translucent watercolour, appeal to a wide range of collectors. Often described as naive and full of fairytale charm, her clean lines and simplicity of form belie the talented artist behind each brush stroke.

Holly is also a skillful upcycler, bringing out the beauty in unwanted items, and breathing new life into furniture that now graces homes all over the country. With an eye for spotting the unusual, and the skill to transform a piece into something wonderful, Holly is definitely a multi-talented artist.
